UNION TERRITORIES OF INDIA

In this website we are going to learn about union territory of india. Union territories form a part of the skeletal structure of the Republic of India. The Union territories are under direct administrative rule of the central government with its center at the national capital city of Delhi
There are 7 union territories in India:
  • Andaman and Nicobar Islands
  • Chandigarh
  • Daman and Diu
  • Dadra and Nagar Haveli
  • National Capital of Delhi
  • Lakshadweep
  • Puducherry
